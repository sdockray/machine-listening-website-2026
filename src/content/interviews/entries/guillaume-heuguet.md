[Guillaume](https://guillaumeheuguet.com) tells us about his new book [*How Music Changed YouTube](https://www.bloomsbury.com/uk/how-music-changed-youtube-9798765100738/)* (Bloomsbury 2024), and in particular the crucial role played by ‘audio fingerprinting’ in the operation of Content ID, YouTube’s system of automated copyright management. We talk about the ‘mathematics of originality’ and the automation of judgment, along with the political economy of music streaming. Audio fingerprinting, it turns out, is a technology about which the public knows very little, but with major significance for the culture industries.
