We talk with [*Jonathan*](https://sterneworks.org/about/) and [*Elena*](http://elenarazlogova.org/) about their [*collaborative work*](https://sterneworks.org/wp-content/uploads/2021/05/Sterne-Razlogova-Tuning.pdf) on AI and the automation of music mastering, with a particular focus on Montreal-based platform [*LANDR*](https://join.landr.com/landr-studio/). But the conversation inevitably drifts towards much bigger questions on the politics of automation and machine listening.
